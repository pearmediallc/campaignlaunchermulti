Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions

How does the platform work? +

Our platform connects to your existing marketing accounts and uses AI to optimize campaigns across all channels. Simply connect your accounts, set your goals, and let our algorithms handle the rest.

What platforms are supported? +

We support all major marketing platforms including Facebook, Instagram, Google Ads, LinkedIn, Twitter, TikTok, and more. New integrations are added regularly.

Is there a free trial? +

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to start.

How secure is my data? +

We use bank-level encryption and are SOC 2 compliant. Your data is never shared with third parties and you maintain full ownership of all your campaign data.

Can I cancel anytime? +

Absolutely! There are no long-term contracts. You can cancel or change your plan at any time from your dashboard.
